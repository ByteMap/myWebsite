My Hobbies

Just like everyone else, having hobbies is an important essential. For me, my hobbies help me through forgetting about things that stresses me out so that I can unwind and reset myself. Additionally, some of my hobbies had expanded my knowledge on topics that I did not know that I would explore. Below is a handful of hobbies that I have picked up over the years.
Watching anime and reading manga is probably my favorite pastime right now. Both of these hobbies never fail to entertain me as there is not only a vast selection available to choose from, but also they ultimately visualize different stories for me. While reading or watching the stories presented to me, I end up being isolated from the problems plagued by reality, and instead inside the fictional world of those stories. These two hobbies essentially act as a cleanser for the stress and anxiety that I accumulate on a daily basis and allow for myself to reset. There have been many instances where these two hobbies of mine have allowed me to calm down and think more clearly, which is why I see them as my most important and enjoyable hobbies.
