About Me
Hi there! I'm Andy, a Junior Software Engineer.
I graduated from the University of California, Santa Cruz with a B.S. in Computer Science during the summer of 2020.
I really enjoy programming as it gives me a great sense of satisfaction every time I get something to work, and understand why it works. Programming to me is like working on a massive jigsaw puzzle with each piece being something that I will have to learn whether they be new technologies or methodologies. By piecing everything together, something magnificent can then be created.
For the past year, I had the honor of working at the UCSC Genomics Institute as a Full Stack Web Developer for the Dockstore project. Not only was I able to learn more about the industry that I was headed into, but I was also able to learn about new coding practices, methodologies, and technologies. My time at the Genomics Institute had ultimately given me more experienced than I had hoped for in order to start off my career.
